Meet the Woman
who styles you

"I started this because every woman in Assam deserves to feel beautiful — without travelling to the city."

Hi, I'm Mrinmoyee — the heart behind Sisters Stylo Studio. I grew up watching women in our community struggle to find quality ethnic wear without spending hours travelling to big cities. So in Moranhat, I started something small with a big dream — to bring beautiful, curated fashion directly to your WhatsApp.

What started as a passion project has grown into a boutique that ships across India and the world. Every piece I list is something I would personally wear or gift to someone I love. That's my promise to you.

Shipping & FAQs

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

Within India — 3 to 7 business days depending on your location. International orders — 10 to 20 business days. We use trusted courier services and share tracking details once dispatched.

We accept exchanges within 3 days of delivery if there is a manufacturing defect or size issue. Please WhatsApp us with photos of the item immediately. Note: sale items are non-returnable.

We accept all UPI apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfers (NEFT/IMPS), and online banking. We do NOT accept Cash on Delivery (COD). Payment must be confirmed before dispatch.

Yes! We ship worldwide. International shipping charges apply based on destination country and package weight. WhatsApp us your address to get a shipping quote before ordering.

Check our Size Guide (available on this page) for detailed measurements. If you're between sizes, we always recommend sizing up. You can also WhatsApp us your measurements and we'll suggest the best fit.

For some items yes! WhatsApp us with your requirements and we'll let you know what's available. Custom orders may take a few extra days depending on stock availability.
